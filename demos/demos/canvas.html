Demo/About/Documentation HTML5 canvas

Webshim Lib for the feature canvas implements one simple jQuery-method .getContext() on Top of excanvas.

$.webshims.ready('canvas', function(){ var ctx = $('#my-canvas').getContext('2d'); ctx.fillStyle = "rgb(200,0,0)"; ctx.fillRect (10, 10, 55, 50); ctx.fillStyle = "rgba(0, 0, 200, 0.5)"; ctx.fillRect (30, 30, 55, 50); });
